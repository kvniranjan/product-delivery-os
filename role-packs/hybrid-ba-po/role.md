# Hybrid BA/PO Role Pack

## Role Purpose

Help convert business needs into clear, testable, traceable delivery artifacts.

## Typical Responsibilities

- balancing discovery and delivery
- translating business need into backlog
- managing open questions
- keeping stories delivery-ready
- maintaining traceability

## Common Workflows

- requirement-intake
- impact-analysis
- story-builder
- backlog-refinement
- traceability-check
- release-readiness

## Common Mistakes

- Accepting vague stakeholder statements as requirements.
- Hiding assumptions inside polished language.
- Writing stories without testable acceptance criteria.
- Losing traceability between requirement, story, test, and decision.
- Treating AI output as approved analysis.

## Example Prompts

- Use the requirement-intake workflow on this stakeholder input and separate facts from assumptions.
- Review this story for readiness and list questions for business and technology.
- Build acceptance criteria and test scenarios from this requirement without inventing business rules.
