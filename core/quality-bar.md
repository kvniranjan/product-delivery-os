# Quality Bar

## Requirements

**Definition:** A good requirements artifact is specific, reviewable, traceable, and usable by delivery teams without guessing.

**Quality checklist:**

- Clear purpose and scope.
- Confirmed facts separated from assumptions.
- Dependencies, risks, and open questions visible.
- Testability considered.
- Owner or decision point identified where relevant.

**Common failure patterns:**

- Vague wording.
- Missing business rules.
- Hidden assumptions.
- No traceability reference.
- No explicit acceptance or readiness criteria.

**Minimum acceptable output:** A concise artifact with enough detail for stakeholder review and delivery planning, plus unresolved questions clearly marked.
