# Release Readiness Checklist

- Do not recommend Go if critical defects, missing signoff, or unresolved production risks exist.
- Identify residual risk.
- Separate readiness facts from recommendation.
- Confirmed facts are separated from assumptions.
- Dependencies are visible.
- Risks are specific.
- Open questions are actionable.
- Output can be reviewed by business and technology stakeholders.
- Traceability references are included where applicable.
